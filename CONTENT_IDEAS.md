## ðŸ’Ž PRO TIPS FOR MAXIMUM REACH

1. **First 3 seconds matter**: Hook viewers immediately
2. **Captions start strong**: First line should make them want to read more
3. **Call-to-action**: Every post should have one (comment, save, share, follow)
4. **Respond quickly**: Engage with comments in first 60 minutes
5. **Cross-post to Stories**: Share feed posts to stories for double visibility
6. **Save-worthy content**: Focus on educational/useful content people want to reference later
7. **Consistency > Perfection**: Better to post regularly than wait for perfect content
8. **Test and analyze**: Try different formats and track what performs best
