- #instadaily

## üìà Hashtag Testing & Optimization

### Monthly Hashtag Audit
1. Track which hashtags drive most engagement
2. Use Instagram Insights ‚Üí "Discovered from Hashtags"
3. Rotate out underperforming hashtags
4. Test 3-5 new hashtags per week

### A/B Testing Strategy
- **Post A:** Use high-reach focused hashtags
- **Post B:** Use niche-focused hashtags
- Compare: Reach vs. Engagement rate
- Adapt strategy based on results

### Seasonal Updates
- Update hashtags quarterly
- Add trending seasonal tags (e.g., #BackToSchool in Aug/Sept)
- Remove outdated or declining hashtags

## üí° Pro Hashtag Tips

1. **Mix Popular + Niche:** Don't use only high-reach; balance is key
2. **Front-load Branded:** Put your branded hashtags first
3. **Hide in Comments:** Some prefer putting hashtags in first comment (slight engagement boost)
4. **Use All 30 Spots:** Instagram allows 30, but 15-25 is optimal for engagement
5. **CamelCase for Accessibility:** #LanguageLearning not #languagelearning (helps screen readers)
6. **Create a Library:** Save sets of hashtags in Notes app for quick copy-paste
7. **Monitor Competitors:** See what successful accounts in your niche use
8. **Update Based on Performance:** Review monthly, keep what works
9. **Avoid Hashtag Stuffing in Caption:** Breaks flow; add at end or in comment
10. **Language-Specific Posts:** When posting about a specific language, include that language's hashtags
